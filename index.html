Our Offerings

VSafe proudly delivers the first-ever AI-powered security solution that harnesses cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning to set new safety standards in corporate transportation.

  • Real-Time Notifications for Immediate Action

    When suspicious activity is detected, our system instantly alerts the cab owner and admin manager. This timely notification allows for rapid response and intervention, ensuring the safety and security of both passengers and drivers.

  • Facial Recognition System

    VSafe's state-of-the-art facial recognition technology securely verifies the identities of drivers and passengers, simplifying the ride approval process without the need for traditional OTP verification. This enhances security while providing a seamless boarding experience.

  • Route Optimization for Efficiency

    Our intelligent route optimizer uses advanced algorithms to analyze traffic and road conditions, ensuring the shortest and most efficient paths. This not only reduces travel times but also enhances fuel efficiency, lowering operational costs for corporate fleets.

  • Continuous Surveillance and Anomaly Detection

    Our advanced monitoring system employs machine learning models to continuously detect potential threats, including signs of sexual misconduct, violence, or unauthorized individuals entering the cab. This ensures proactive safety measures are in place at all times.
